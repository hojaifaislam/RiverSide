Best Price Guarantee

Starting 1st January 2012, we guarantee that prices found on our website are of the best available out there. Find a lower price elsewhere and we guarantee that your first night is free. Find out more benefits when you book direct with us. Learn about our guarantee.

And, if you find a better rate for Riverside Majestic Hotel within the next 24 hours of confirming your reservation, your first night is free and we will match the rate for the remaining nights.
