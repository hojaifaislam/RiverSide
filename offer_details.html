Facalities

  2. Kuching City Tour
    • Duration : 3 Hour
    • Tour offered are based in Seat In Coach basis.
    • Kuching, the Capital of Sarawak, is a cosmopolitan city and this is reflected in the faces on the street. Its city center is well preserved with ornate Chinese Temples, The Astana, City Tower, many fine examples of colonial-style architecture a beautiful waterfront and a number of interesting museums, including the famous Sarawak Museum & Cat Museum. Other places of interest include the famous landmark of the City, the Cat Monument, Market, Main Bazaar, China Town, Old State Mosque & etc. Kuching is a good destination to shop for souvenir such as woodcarvings, "Pua Khumbu" with native design, Pepper products and Sarawak pottery. Sarawak Bird’s Nest and Local Products are some of the famous souvenirs which can be found in Kuching.
  3. Sunset Cruise
    • Time : 05:30 PM to 07:00 PM
    • A cruise along the Sarawak River is a relaxing way to enjoy the various phases of the city, retracing the trip James Brooke made when he first arrived in Sarawak. Take in the sights of the Waterfront, Brooke Dockyard, the 100-year old Malay Kampung, Fort Maghemites, Astana, Square Tower and the houses and mansions of Kuching's affluent society. This living scenery gives you a snapshot of the history and lifestyles of Kuching.
